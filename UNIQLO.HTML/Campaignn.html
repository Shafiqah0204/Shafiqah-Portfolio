OUR CAMPAIGN

The Uniqlo CSR program is the initiative of the campaign called "From Street to Shelter" to help realize the urgent dressing needs of the homeless and underprivileged society. This initiative espouses dignity, comfort and hope in distributing the warm and functional clothing of Uniqlo LifeWear line.

The event is aimed towards giving the people high quality basic clothing which many people are in need of so that they can have clothes to wear to keep themselves warm, especially in the winter seasons.
